INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON INDUSTRY 5.0 [ICI 5.0 2024]

The International Conference on Industry 5.0 (ICI 5.0) is a significant event bringing together engineers, academics, scientists, and industry professionals from around the world. Industry 5.0 emphasizes human-centric practices and sustainability, it complements Industry 4.0 by emphasizing innovation for industry resilience. As the successor to ICI 4.0 and ICAICT, this conference focuses on shaping the future of industry. Offering a collaborative space for knowledge exchange, ICI 5.0 fosters global collaboration and addresses multifaceted challenges, cultivating intelligent solutions for the future. Submitted papers undergo a rigorous evaluation by the esteemed technical committee, ensuring the highest standards of quality and intellectual rigor. The conference offers a unique opportunity for researchers to connect and engage in discussions on practical methods for solving diverse problems with individuals actively immersed in these fields. The conference seeks to inspire all participants, including researchers, practitioners, and academics, to actively pursue additional research initiatives. It aims to foster an environment that encourages continuous research and learning across various academic domains. Organized by Saraswati College of Engineering, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai, the International Conference on Industry 5.0 is dedicated to advancing innovations in the evolving landscape of the fifth industrial revolution.

About SCOE

Welcome to the Saraswati College of Engineering

Saraswati College of Engineering (SCOE), founded in 2004 by Hon. Prithviraj Deshmukh and Hon. Mrs. Vrushali Deshmukh, is a distinguished engineering institute committed to academic excellence. SCOE is recognized by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to University of Mumbai. The college, nestled in the scenic Kharghar Hills, is accredited with an A+ grade by NAAC and holds NBA accreditation for two undergraduate programs, showcasing its commitment to excellence in engineering education. SCOE offers a range of undergraduate courses in Civil, Mechanical, Automobile, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, CSE- DS, and CSE-AIML, along with postgraduate programs in Civil. With a focus on providing state-of-the-art technical education, SCOE aims to equip aspiring engineers of the 21st century with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the professional world. The college is dedicated to enhancing the employability of its students, bridging the gap between industry and academia.

    • Objective of the Conference

    Following the footsteps of the successful ICI 4.0 and ICAICT conferences, the upcoming International Conference on Industry 5.0 (ICI 5.0) aspires to advance understanding and collaboration within the realm of Industry 5.0. With the emergence of various technologies shaping the modern industrial landscape, ICI 5.0 aims to raise awareness about the transformative potential of Industry 5.0, the latest digital industrial technology. ICI 5.0 aims to equip participants with the competence required to compete in the dynamic global market, emphasizing the dynamic and high-scale nature of the present and future market scenarios. In alignment with previous objectives, ICI 5.0 also encourages faculty engagement in extensive research, aspiring to create an environment that promotes research across various fields within Industry 5.0. By fostering a culture of innovation and learning, ICI 5.0 aims to contribute to ongoing advancements and prepare stakeholders for the challenges and opportunities presented by the fifth industrial revolution.
