Objective of the Conference

Following the footsteps of the successful ICI 4.0 and ICAICT conferences, the upcoming International Conference on Industry 5.0 (ICI 5.0) aspires to advance understanding and collaboration within the realm of Industry 5.0. With the emergence of various technologies shaping the modern industrial landscape, ICI 5.0 aims to raise awareness about the transformative potential of Industry 5.0, the latest digital industrial technology. ICI 5.0 aims to equip participants with the competence required to compete in the dynamic global market, emphasizing the dynamic and high-scale nature of the present and future market scenarios. In alignment with previous objectives, ICI 5.0 also encourages faculty engagement in extensive research, aspiring to create an environment that promotes research across various fields within Industry 5.0. By fostering a culture of innovation and learning, ICI 5.0 aims to contribute to ongoing advancements and prepare stakeholders for the challenges and opportunities presented by the fifth industrial revolution.
